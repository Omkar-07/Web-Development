Fraud Alert

At JobFinder, your safety and security are our top priorities. We want to alert you to potential fraudulent activities and scams that could affect job seekers and employers alike.

Fraudulent activities can take many forms, including phishing scams, fake job postings, and other deceptive practices. It's crucial to remain vigilant and informed to protect yourself and your personal information.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Verify the authenticity of job offers and employers before providing any personal information.
  • Be cautious of unsolicited communications requesting sensitive details.
  • Report suspicious activities to JobFinder and relevant authorities.
